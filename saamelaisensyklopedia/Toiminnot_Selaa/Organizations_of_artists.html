TekstiThis property is a special property in this wiki. <P align="justify"> Saami artists ha … <P align="justify"> Saami artists had formed Nordic organizations to advance their professional concerns and to promote the Saami identity. <i>Sámi Girječálliid Searvi</i> (SGS; The Association of Saami Writers ) was founded in 1979 in order to promote Saami literature, its writing, publication and translation into other languages. it also aims to improve the economic situation of Saami writers and their to achieve for them a status of equality with other writers in the Nordic countries. It also organizes various courses and seminars, including seminars for writers the results of which have been published as works of literature. <i>Sámi Dáiddačehpiid Searvi</i> (SDS; The Association of Saami Artists ) was founded in 1979. It is an organization of Saami painters and handicraft artists. Its aim is achieve equality at the Nordic level. it has organized art exhibitions and happenings, including <i>Min otná dáidda</i> Our present arts in Tromsø in1980, <i>Sápmi eallá</i> Saamiland Lives , a travelling exhibition in 1981, an exhibition <i>Vi er samer</i> We Are Saamis in the Danish National Museum in Copenhagen in 1992, <i>Sámi Dáidda</i> Saami Arts an exhibition which was put on in Greenland, Panama, Copenhagen and Oslo in 1994, and it has maintained a sales exhibition In Karasjok since 1985. The association published an art journal called <i>Čehppodat Áiti</i> in 1981. <i>Sámi Musihkkariid Searvi</i> The Association of Saami Musicians was founded in 1982 is a professional organization of juoiggus artists, musicians and lyricists. Its aim is to improve the work opportunities for musical artists. <i>Sámi Teahtersearvi</i> The Saami Theatre Association was established in 1980; it is a cooperative organ promoting the interests of Saami theatre workers and actors across national frontiers.</P> <BR> [[Organizations|Organizations]] <BR>[[Organizations|Organizations]] <BR>  +
