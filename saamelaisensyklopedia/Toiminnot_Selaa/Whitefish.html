Whitefish vary in length from 20 to 55 cm and generally weight 0.2-1.5 kg, rarely exceeding 5 kg. They spawn in autumn on the shallow gravel beds of rivers or lakes, and the eggs hatch the following spring. They feed off zooplankton, bottom-dwelling animals, insects and their larvae.
